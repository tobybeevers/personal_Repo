In 2013 I took a decision to try something new, staying in the world of food I joined Entegra

You can read more about what drove my decisions in my Blog Post

  • 2013
    OPERATIONS MANAGER
    Collaborated directly with leading foodservice brands to deliver customised service solutions aimed at optimising purchasing processes, reducing operational costs, and enhancing efficiency.
  • 2014
    ePSL ACCOUNT MANAGER
    Transitioned into the systems domain, providing operational support for customer systems. Responsibilities included system setup, user training, and seamless implementation to ensure client success.
  • 2017
    SYSTEMS PROJECT MANAGER
    Acted as the primary liaison between business stakeholders and the technical development team. Functioned as a hybrid Product Owner and Project Manager, overseeing budgets, deliverables, and project timelines to drive successful outcomes.
  • 2018
    HEAD OF SYSTEMS UK
    Led the day-to-day operations of systems and business processes across the UK and Ireland, later expanding oversight to eight countries by 2020. Managed a team of two direct reports and approximately 25 indirect reports, while collaborating with external development partners to deliver the annual technical roadmap within an Agile/Scrum framework.
  • 2021
    HEAD OF EUROPE DATA & IT
    Assumed leadership of technical and data development initiatives across eight European countries. Reported directly to the European CEO and contributed to strategic decision-making as part of the Europe Leadership Team, driving innovation and alignment in data and system development.
  • 2024
    DIR TECH & DATA
    Advanced into my first director-level role, leveraging extensive experience to lead technology and data strategies, foster innovation, and deliver impactful business solutions.
